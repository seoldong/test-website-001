We'd like to share our farm's story of stepping away from the fast, complex city life and embracing **Minimalist Living** close to nature. Minimalism is more than just owning fewer things; it's a way of focusing on the essence of life.

## üåæ The Core of Farm Minimalism

Life on the farm naturally encourages the practice of minimalism. Here are the three core values we emphasize:

1.  **Production over Consumption:** Instead of buying necessities, we **grow our own** ingredients directly from the land. This process gives us a deep understanding of 'what we truly need.'
2.  **Nature's Rhythm:** We align our lives with the **rhythm of nature**, following the changing seasons and the rising and setting of the sun. This frees us from the stress of artificial schedules typical of city life.
3.  **Less is More Abundant:** As our surroundings and possessions become simpler, our **time and mental energy** become richer. This energy allows us to focus on creative activities or true rest.

## ü™¥ Small Tips to Start

Even if you can't move to a farm right away, you can practice a farm-style minimalist life in your daily routine.

* **Digital Detox:** Set aside a specific time each day to step away from your smartphone and computer, and instead, look at the **sky outside your window or a potted plant**.
* **Simple Ingredients:** Increase your intake of healthy meals made with **single ingredients** instead of complex processed foods. Garden vegetables or seasonal fruits are excellent choices.
* **Declutter Your Wardrobe:** Tidy up clothes you haven't worn in a year, and only keep items that are **durable and highly versatile**.

We invite you to pause for a moment in this complicated world and experience the **beauty of slowness** that nature teaches us.
