## üçπ 3 Vitality-Boosting Detox Juice Recipes

All recipes can be made using a juicer or a high-powered blender.

### 1. Green Energy Cleanse

* **Key Benefits:** Detoxification, Immune System Boost
* **Ingredients:**
    * Farm Kale or Spinach: 1 cup
    * Apple: 1/2 (for added sweetness)
    * Cucumber: 1/2
    * Ginger: Small piece (about 1 cm)
    * Lemon: 1/4 (Essential)
* **Instructions:** Wash all ingredients thoroughly, chop them into appropriate sizes, place them in a blender, and blend with 1/2 cup of water.

### 2. Beet Blood Booster

* **Key Benefits:** Improved Blood Circulation, Fatigue Relief
* **Ingredients:**
    * Beet (peeled): 1/4
    * Carrot: 1
    * Celery: 2 stalks
    * Orange: 1
* **Instructions:** Chop the hard beet into small pieces, peel the orange, and blend them with the remaining ingredients. The earthiness of the beet will be neutralized by the freshness of the orange.

### 3. Golden Pumpkin Warm-up

* **Key Benefits:** Digestion Promotion, Antioxidant Action
* **Ingredients:**
    * Cooked Sweet/Winter Squash: 1/2 cup
    * Banana: 1/2 (for added smoothness)
    * Cinnamon Powder: A dash
    * Water or Almond Milk: 1 cup
* **Instructions:** Steam and cool the squash, then place all ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth. (It can also be warmed up and enjoyed hot.)
