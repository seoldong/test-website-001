Lavender is an all-purpose herb that has been used medicinally since ancient times.

1.  **Stress and Anxiety Relief:** Linalool, a main component of lavender, stabilizes the nervous system, lowering anxiety and stress levels.
2.  **Sleep Induction:** Placing a drop of lavender oil on your pillow before bedtime can induce deep, comfortable sleep, helping to alleviate insomnia.
3.  **Headache and Migraine Relief:** Inhaling the scent of lavender or massaging the oil onto the temples is effective in reducing tension headaches.
4.  **Skin Soothing Effect:** Applying it to minor burns, insect bites, or acne spots helps reduce inflammation and promotes skin regeneration.
5.  **Mood Boost and Focus Enhancement:** The scent of lavender can inject vitality and clear the mind when you feel down or exhausted.
6.  **Pain Reduction:** When experiencing muscle or joint pain, mixing it with massage oil can provide an analgesic effect.
7.  **Natural Insect Repellent:** Its scent is disliked by mosquitoes and pests, making it useful to keep indoors or during outdoor activities.
