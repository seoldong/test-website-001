### Instructions

1.  Mix the oat flour and honey thoroughly in a bowl.
2.  Gradually add warm water or milk until you achieve a thick consistency that won't easily run off.
3.  Apply a generous layer of the mask to a clean face, avoiding the eye and mouth areas.
4.  After 10 to 15 minutes, gently massage and wash it off with lukewarm water.
